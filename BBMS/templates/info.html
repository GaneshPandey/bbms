Raktadata as its name suggests is a web-app that allows people to search for blood.



Donors ie. people who wish to donate blood register and create their profile in the website.



Anyone who requires blood fill-up the request form. After the request form has been submitted then the web-app searches suitable donor from the database and texts them the request and the requesters mobile phone number. The donor then calls back the requester.



Hope this app helps you.
